About Us

In Robotics club

Part of what we do is build robots, repair robots, code, raise money for our team, alongside with many other things. The focus of Robotics club is working towards the goal of winning championship at the Fright Frenzie robotics competition. Our purpose is to explore new things in the world of software and hardware, expand our skills, and help our teammates in their robotics journey.

Before our current journey, only about 6 people would show up to competitions but each one had their own role and we did it well. During the "Rover Ruckus" competition we were able to pull through with a preliminary win which was a greay accomplishment for us and we tried to keep that same level of deducation even after we lost our next competition.

We come together twice a week to discuss ideas and opinions on how to improve our robot in order to be able to get ready and win the Freight Frezie competition. We are located at a high school in The Bronx, New York.
